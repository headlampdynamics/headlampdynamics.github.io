What I Deliver

① Control Optimisation

“Make the loops you already own work harder.”

  • Higher throughput, tighter quality, lower energy spend.
  • Mine historical data, flag under‑performing loops.
  • Lightweight models (SINDy / regression) to expose the gap.
  • Fuzzy-logic tweaks or set‑point re‑tuning on existing PLC/DCS — no new hardware.

Typical wins: 2–10 % energy cut, smoother line startup, fewer operator overrides.

② Health Monitoring & Fault Prediction

“Know a failure is coming before the alarms light up.”

  • Create a digital twin of each critical asset from live data.
  • Track critical slowing‑down indicators (variance, autocorrelation).
  • Push simple traffic‑light alerts to the control room or CMMS.

Typical wins: Catch bearing wear or valve stiction days to weeks sooner; schedule downtime on your terms.
